Disneyplus.com/begin



Disneyplus.com/begin: Disney Plus is an online streaming service that is subscription-based from Disney and will feature content that comes from Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar and Disney. Sign in to a Disney+ account and enter the 8-digit code you see on your TV to activate the Disney Plus channel on your device.


Sign In to your Disney+ account


  • Using the Disney+ App or Website:
    • Launch the Disney+ app on your smart TV or TV-connected device.
    • Select “Log In.”
    • Enter the email address and password associated with your Disney+ account.
    • Click “Log In” to confirm1.
  • Using a Computer or Mobile Browser:
    • Visit DisneyPlus.com from a computer or mobile browser.
    • Enter your email address and password.
    • Click “Log In” to access your Disney+ account.

Disney Plus 8 Digit Activation Code

The 8-digit activation code is a unique identifier used to activate services or devices. For example, when setting up Disney Plus on your TV or streaming device, you’ll need to enter this code on the activation page to link your account and start streaming.


Activate Disney Plus on your Device


  • Visit the Activation Page: Using your web browser, go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Enter the Code: The activation link will open the activation page. Enter the 8-digit code displayed on your TV or connected device.
  • Activate: Click on the “Activate” button.
  • Login: Enter your Disney Plus login credentials (email address and password).
  • Start Streaming: Finally, press the “Login” button, and you’re all set! Enjoy streaming Disney Plus on any compatible device.

How to activate Disney Plus on Roku?


To activate Disney Plus on Roku, follow these steps:

  • Turn on your Roku and press the Home button on your remote.
  • Navigate to Streaming Channels or Search.
  • In the search bar, type Disney Plus.
  • From the search results, select the Disney Plus app.
  • Click on Add Channel. The app will download and install on your Roku.
  • Once installed, you can select Go to channel to launch the Disney+ app.
  • With the Disney+ app open on your Roku, choose the Log In option.
  • On a separate device like your phone or computer, open a web browser and visit disneyplus.com login/begin.
  • Search for "activate" or find instructions related to activating Disney+ on a device.
  • You should find instructions mentioning Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Keep the web browser open on your separate device.
  • On your Roku, enter your Disney+ login credentials (email and password).
  • You might see an activation code displayed on your TV screen.
  • Go back to the web browser on your other device and navigate to the activation instructions you found on disneyplus.
  • Enter the activation code displayed on your Roku into the website.
  • You should be signed in to your Disney+ account on your Roku.
